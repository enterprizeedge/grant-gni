# AI-Edge Health — Impact (excerpt from a funded proposal)

[section: impact]
Our previous project delivered a TRL-6 edge-AI inference module for portable ultrasound,
validated in two clinical sites. The impact pathway connected each project result to a
measurable outcome: a 32% reduction in scan-to-triage time, adoption commitments from
three regional health authorities, and an open dataset released under a FAIR data plan.

Key practices that evaluators rewarded:
- A quantified baseline and target for every expected outcome.
- A dissemination, exploitation and communication (DEC) plan with named channels, KPIs and
  a dedicated exploitation lead.
- An IP and standardisation strategy linking results to two CEN/CENELEC workshop agreements.
- A risk register tying each impact risk to a concrete mitigation and an owner.
