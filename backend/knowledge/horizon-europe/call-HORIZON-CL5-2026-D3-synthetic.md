# Call context (funder's strategic intent)

## Expected outcome
Projects are expected to contribute to ALL of the following outcomes:
- Demonstrated cost reduction of green hydrogen production at industrial scale,
  with a credible pathway to below EUR 3/kg by 2030.
- Increased European supply-chain resilience for electrolyser components.
- Validated integration of electrolysis with variable renewable electricity,
  reducing curtailment.

## Scope
Proposals should demonstrate, in a relevant industrial environment, an integrated
green-hydrogen production system coupling renewable electricity with advanced
electrolysis. Activities should address durability, dynamic operation under
variable load, and lifecycle sustainability. Consortia should include at least one
industrial off-taker from a hard-to-abate sector (steel, cement, chemicals).

## Strategic framing (why the funder cares)
This topic operationalises the REPowerEU and EU Hydrogen Strategy goals. Evaluators
will reward proposals that explicitly connect technical results to EU-level energy
sovereignty, decarbonisation of hard-to-abate sectors, and industrial competitiveness
against non-EU electrolyser manufacturers. Strong proposals quantify the contribution
to the 2030 renewable-hydrogen targets and articulate the policy relevance, not just
the technology.

## Cross-cutting priorities to address
- Open science and FAIR data management.
- Gender dimension in the research/innovation content.
- "Do No Significant Harm" and lifecycle/environmental sustainability.
- A credible exploitation and standardisation strategy.
