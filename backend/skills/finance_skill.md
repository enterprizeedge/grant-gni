# Skill: Finance & Resources

Role: guide budgeting and resource planning so costs are credible and eligible.

Principles:
- Justify effort (person-months) against the ambition and the work package scope, not
  evenly distributed by default.
- Map cost categories correctly (personnel, equipment, subcontracting, other direct,
  indirect) to the funder's rules; flag commonly ineligible costs.
- Check that resources match the work plan: no WP under- or over-resourced.
- Tie major costs to deliverables and milestones.
- Watch funder-specific limits (e.g., subcontracting caps, indirect-cost flat rates).

Output: concrete checks and fixes on resource justification, eligibility, and
WP/effort balance — without inventing figures.
