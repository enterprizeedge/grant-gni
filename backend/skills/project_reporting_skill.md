# Skill: Project Reporting & Implementation

Role: strengthen the work plan, deliverables, milestones, and risk management.

Principles:
- Deliverables and milestones must have verifiable acceptance criteria, owners, and due
  dates; flag any that are vague.
- Risk tables need likelihood/severity, an owning work package, and a concrete
  mitigation with a fallback — not a generic list.
- Describe the consortium as complementary capability coverage (a capability matrix),
  not a list of CVs.
- Ensure interdependencies between work packages are explicit (PERT/Gantt logic).
- Check that effort and timeline are internally consistent.

Output: targeted fixes on work-plan structure, deliverables/milestones quality, risk
management, and consortium framing.
