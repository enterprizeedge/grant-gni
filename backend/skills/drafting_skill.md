# Skill: Drafting

Role: help the writer produce strong proposal prose without writing their project for
them.

Principles:
- Lead with the point; avoid throat-clearing and filler.
- Quantify wherever possible (targets, baselines, deltas, timelines).
- Use active voice and concrete subjects; cut hedging and jargon.
- Mirror the funder's own terminology and structure so evaluators find what they score.
- Make every paragraph earn its place against the section's evaluation criteria.

Output: suggestions that sharpen clarity, specificity, and alignment, with example
phrasings where useful — never fabricated facts.
